Data Deletion Instructions

At Bliitz, we respect your privacy and are committed to ensuring that you can control your data. If you wish to delete your data, please follow the instructions below:

How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Open the app and go to the profile page, then acess your account menu.
  2. Click on the "Delete Account" button and confirm the action.
  3. Your account and all your data will be permanently deleted.

Contact Support

If you need assistance or want to request data deletion, please contact our support team at tech.bliitzapp@gmail.com.

Data Export

If you'd like to export your data before deletion, you can request a copy by contacting us at tech.bliitzapp@gmail.com.

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to us!
